Karol Krasnowolski MSci. | Founder, CEO LinkedIn

Award-winner for his entrepreneurship, Karol graduated at the top of his cohort from City, St. George’s University, with Master's First Class Honours in Biomedical Science. His area of expertise is molecular genetics with emphasis on the mechanisms of DNA replication. Alongside his research, he has a record of building and scaling several successful e-commerce projects.

Experienced in applying automation to these fields, he is excited for the prospects of AI to improve the rate of scientific discovery and also to save time for business owners.

An early adopter and enthusiast of artificial intelligence, Karol recalls using the first large language model he experimented with, circa 2008, dubbed “Cleverbot”.
