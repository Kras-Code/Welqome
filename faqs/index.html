WelQome | FAQs

How will this service benefit my business?

AI reception delivers intelligent 24/7 call handling, reduces missed calls, and books appointments while you sleep. Find out more about the benefits here.

Does this service require changing phone numbers?

No—keep your existing business number and set up call forwarding with your mobile/provider. We can forward all calls instantly or only missed calls to your AI receptionist.

Can you integrate our existing systems?

Yes. We offer automated integrations with 1,000+ tools (e.g., calendars, CRMs, help desks). See supported integrations here.

Will our customers know they are talking to AI?

Our agents are trained for warm, natural conversations—most callers won’t realise. You can also choose to disclose that they’re speaking with an AI assistant.

How is pricing structured?

A one-time setup fee to tailor the AI to your brand and systems, plus a small monthly retainer for upkeep. Costs depend on your requirements and scale—share a budget and we’ll align where possible.

What constitutes fair usage?

Fair usage limits are defined to ensure quality and reliability for all clients. Read our Fair Usage policy here.
