The Facts

WelQome answers after hours to always capture revenue around-the-clock:

Here are the facts

  • Speed to lead

    ~7× higher qualification odds[1]

    Responding within <60 min materially increases conversion efficiency versus slower follow-up.

  • Customer expectations

    77% expect immediate interaction;[4]

    ~75% expect 24/7 availability[5]

    Immediate response aligns with normative expectations and reduces abandonment.

  • Missed calls

    >80% won’t leave voicemail;[2]

    1 in 5 won’t call back[3]

    Live pickup prevents first-contact drop-off and preserves otherwise lost demand.

Sources
  • [1] Oldroyd, McElheran & Elkington. Harvard Business Review (2011), “The Short Life of Online Sales Leads”: contacting within 1 hour ≈7× more likely to qualify.
  • [2] SellCell (2023), “Voicemail Statistics”: ~20% of callers leave a voicemail.
  • [3] Aircall (citing BT Business), “Missed Calls: The Real Impact on Your Business”: ~20% won’t make a second call if the first isn’t answered.
  • [4] Salesforce — “7 Principles for a Better Customer Experience” / State of the Connected Customer: 77% expect immediate interaction.
  • [5] Document360 (2025) CX research roundup: ~75% of customers expect brands to offer 24×7 service.
  • [6] Salesforce — State of Service/Connected Customer: 88% say good service increases likelihood of repurchase.
