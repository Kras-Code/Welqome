From initial consultation to deployment, our workflow ensures a seamless integration of AI reception into your business.

1. Needs Assessment

We begin with a discussion of your business needs, customer interaction points and desired outcomes.

→

2. Solution Proposal & Agreement

We outline the AI receptionist automation plan, including integrations tailored to your business.

→

3. Bespoke AI Workflow Development (7–14 Days)

Our engineers build and customise your AI receptionist pipeline.

→

4. Internal Testing & Quality Assurance

We simulate real call and scheduling scenarios to ensure accuracy, reliability and GDPR compliance.

→

5. Deployment & Onboarding

WelQome goes live for your business. We provide training and ensure smooth integration with your daily operations.

→

6. Ongoing Optimisation & Support

Post-launch, we monitor performance and provide 24/7 support.
